## Headlines

- **Northshore office vacancy continues to tighten.** St. Tammany Parish office space is seeing sustained demand from professional services, healthcare, and insurance tenants relocating from New Orleans metro. Class A/B space in Mandeville and Covington is increasingly constrained, with fewer availabilities below 1,000 SF.

- **Metairie multi-family rents pushing higher, but new supply is a watch item.** Several new apartment developments are under construction or recently delivered in the Metairie/Kenner corridor. Current occupancy remains healthy (93–95%), but absorption of new units will be the key metric to watch over the next 2–3 quarters.

- **Industrial demand on the Northshore remains strong.** Logistics, distribution, and light manufacturing users continue to seek developed industrial lots in St. Tammany Parish. Improved industrial space is scarce — most activity is in land sales for build-to-suit. Gulf South Commerce Park is well-positioned for this demand.

- **Mandeville townhome/HOA market stable.** Sale prices for townhomes in HOA communities like Lakeside Village continue their gradual upward trend. Rental demand for townhome-style units remains strong, with limited inventory keeping vacancy near zero for quality communities.
