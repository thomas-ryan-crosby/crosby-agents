## Headlines

- **Mandeville Lake Apartments rent roll loaded — 35 active leases averaging $1,421/mo; priced at-market on 2BR, modestly below on 1BR.** The highest-priority intelligence gap from the April 2 run is now closed. Weighted average across the 35 active units is $1,421/mo on roughly $49,745 monthly rental revenue. By bedroom: 1BR (n=11) averages $1,149/mo vs. Mandeville market avg of $1,275 (approximately 10% below market, $126/unit upside); 2BR (n=19) averages $1,498/mo vs. market $1,461+ (at or slightly above market); 3BR (n=5) averages $1,730/mo (limited comp data). Given the +13.6% Mandeville market rent growth YoY, the 1BR pool is the clearest rate-review opportunity in the portfolio.

- **Gulf South Commerce Park momentum continues — Steel 12 construction underway.** Blue Steel Development's $22M, 200,000 SF multi-tenant warehouse ("Steel 12") remains under construction after the March 2026 groundbreaking. Love's Travel Stop at I-12/LA-1088 is now in its second month of operations. No new parcel transactions reported mid-month, but the corridor continues to attract interest per St. Tammany EDC channels. Blue Steel's future asking rates will become the first true I-12 industrial comp.

- **Sanctuary Building 1 blended rate holds at $25.22/SF across 10 active leases on 9,688 SF.** With Suite 302 (Security National, $29.50/SF) set to vacate June 30, and Suite 100 (Fine Southern Products, $36.00/SF, MTM) no longer in the active lease set, Building 1 current active revenue is $20,359/mo ($244,310/yr) on 9,688 SF — a blended $25.22/SF/yr, still 15–48% above Northshore asking-rate market ($17–$22/SF). Premium is intact and justified by full-service structure and near-full occupancy, but retention of Bayou CPR and Nirvana Massage (both with July 4 notice deadlines) is now even more critical to preserving the blended rate.

- **Metairie multi-family: still soft, still below market — no change since early April.** Metairie Lake (108 units) remains listed with entry rates near $1,000/mo against a city 1BR average of $1,118 and 2BR of $1,342. Metairie Plaza (66 units) is competitively priced at $1,200–$1,735 across 2–3BR. New 150-unit luxury pipeline in Metairie reconfirmed; delivery expected 2027. Near-term, the sub-market rate positioning protects occupancy but caps rate growth.
