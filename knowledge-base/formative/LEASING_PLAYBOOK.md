## 1. Commercial Leasing — Sanctuary Office Park

### 1.1 Notice & Auto-Renewal Framework

The majority of Sanctuary Office Park leases include auto-renewal clauses with advance notice requirements. **The notice deadline is the date by which Crosby or the tenant must act — after this date, the lease auto-renews on its existing terms.**

| Notice Period | Meaning | Action Required By |
|--------------|---------|-------------------|
| 180 days | Must notify tenant (or receive notice) at least 180 days before termination | Crosby |
| 90 days | Must notify tenant (or receive notice) at least 90 days before termination | Crosby |
| None | No notice required — fixed term or option | N/A |

**Rule of thumb:** Send renewal outreach **30 days before the notice deadline** to give time for negotiation before the clock forces an outcome.

### 1.4 Renewal Offer Standard Terms

When issuing a renewal offer letter, use the following baseline terms unless specific circumstances warrant adjustment:

- **Term:** 2-year renewal preferred; 1-year available for tenants requesting flexibility
- **Escalation:** 3.0% per annum (consistent with existing lease structure)
- **Notice period:** Maintain same notice requirement as existing lease
- **Tenant improvements:** Typically none for renewals of existing buildout; negotiable for modified space
- **Response deadline:** Give tenant 21 days to respond to renewal offer
- **Letter format:** Professional business letter on Crosby Development letterhead (drafted as MD, converted to DOCX for sending)

**Renewal rate calculation (3% increase):**
```
New Year 1 Rate = Current Rate × 1.03
New Year 2 Rate = Year 1 Rate × 1.03
```

### 1.5 Vacancy Response Procedure

When a commercial tenant confirms vacancy (notice received or lease expires):

1. **Day 1 — Confirm vacancy date** and update LEASE_TERMS with vacating flag
2. **Day 1 — Trigger Vacancy Marketing Agent** to generate listing copy
3. **Within 3 days** — produce one-page leasing brief for broker distribution
4. **Within 7 days** — list on LoopNet and CoStar (after approval)
5. **Ongoing** — weekly status update on leasing activity
6. **Prior to vacancy** — schedule inspection and assess space condition

**Suite 302 (Sec National — 655 SF) — June 30, 2026 vacancy:**

### 2.2 Turnover Procedure
1. Resident provides notice (typically 30–60 days)
2. Unit flagged as pending vacancy in rent roll
3. Vacancy Marketing Agent generates updated listing copy
4. Residential Leasing Agent queues renewal outreach for other units with expiring leases

### 2.3 Fair Housing Compliance Language
All listing copy, marketing materials, and tenant communications must include appropriate Fair Housing language. Standard disclaimer:

> *"Crosby Development Company, L.L.C. is an equal opportunity housing provider. We do not discriminate based on race, color, national origin, religion, sex, familial status, disability, or any other protected class under applicable federal, state, or local law."*

This disclaimer is required on all printed brochures, ILS listings, and website copy.

## 3. HOA Leasing — Lakeside Village

Crosby owns 11 lots in Lakeside Village, of which 11 are rented (4 by third-party managers, 7 directly by Crosby). Standard HOA lease terms:

- All leases must comply with Lakeside Village HOA rules and CC&Rs
- Tenant must receive copy of HOA rules at lease signing
- HOA dues paid by Crosby (owner); not passed through to tenant unless explicitly in lease
- Minimum lease term: 12 months (per HOA policy)

## 4. Agent Handoff Protocol

When the Lease Intelligence Agent identifies a renewal action item, it:
1. Produces a lease review summary (MD) — shows all tenants, urgency tiers, recommended actions
2. Produces individual renewal offer letter drafts (MD) for urgent tenants
3. Posts all documents to Dashboard → Documents tab with status: **Pending Review**
4. Ryan reviews, edits if needed, and approves
5. Approved letters are converted to DOCX and sent via Gmail

The Vacancy Marketing Agent activates when:
- LEASE_TERMS shows `vacating: true` OR
- A lease expires without renewal confirmation
