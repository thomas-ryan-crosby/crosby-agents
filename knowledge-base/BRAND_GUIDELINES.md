## Voice & Tone

### Overall Brand Voice
Crosby Development has served the New Orleans metro and Northshore since 1954. The brand voice should reflect:
- **Established trust** — 70+ years of community presence
- **Local pride** — Deep roots in Louisiana culture and community
- **Quality craftsmanship** — Properties built and managed to a higher standard
- **Approachable professionalism** — Warm but credible

### Residential Voice
- Warm, community-focused, lifestyle-oriented
- Emphasize belonging, comfort, and neighborhood character
- Use "home" language — not just "unit" or "apartment"
- Highlight proximity to local Louisiana amenities (lakes, dining, schools)

### Commercial Voice
- Professional, ROI-focused, data-informed
- Emphasize location advantages, accessibility, and tenant success
- Use business language: "your operation," "your team," "your growth"
- Highlight economic incentives and Northshore/metro market advantages

## Fair Housing & Compliance Language

All residential marketing materials MUST include the following:

**Standard Fair Housing Statement:**
> "We are an Equal Housing Opportunity provider. We do not discriminate on the basis of race, color, national origin, religion, sex, familial status, or disability."

**Fair Housing Logo:** Include the Equal Housing Opportunity logo on all print materials.

**Louisiana-Specific Requirements:**
- Do not include language that expresses preferences based on protected classes
- Avoid phrases like "perfect for couples," "great for professionals," "quiet neighborhood" (can imply exclusion)
- Always describe the property and amenities — not the ideal resident

## Messaging Do's and Don'ts

| ✅ Do | ❌ Don't |
|---|---|
| Describe amenities and features | Describe who should live there |
| Use Louisiana/local landmarks as context | Use generic national copy |
| Reference the property's history or community | Overpromise or use superlatives without backup |
| Match tone to asset class (residential vs. commercial) | Use the same copy template for all properties |
| Include specific square footage, pricing, availability | Leave key facts vague |
