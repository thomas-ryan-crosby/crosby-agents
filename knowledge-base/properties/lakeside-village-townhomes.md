## Community Overview

Gated luxury townhome and cottage community in the heart of Mandeville, just steps from the Lake Pontchartrain Causeway. Developed by Crosby Development Company. The community includes 31 townhomes and 14 cottages across three streets: Rue Degas, Rue Esplanade, Rue Renoir, and Rue Monet.

**Crosby Involvement:**
- Partial unit ownership (11 lots owned by LSV/Crosby or Crosby family members)
- Crosby-managed rentals (11 units under Crosby property management)
- Full HOA management for the entire community
