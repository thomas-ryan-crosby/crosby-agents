Operating-cost structure (full-service gross)

All utility charges are paid by the Lessor. There are no common-area charges and no real-estate taxes payable by the Lessee. Janitorial service is provided by the Lessor per the Sanctuary Office Park cleaning schedule.

Repairs & maintenance

Lessor maintains: roof; elevator; building exterior & parking areas; HVAC and plumbing systems; electrical systems (except fixtures); structural components; janitorial per the park schedule.

Lessee maintains: all interior finish surfaces (floors, walls, ceilings); light bulb & fixture replacement; interior window cleaning; kitchen appliances & fixtures. Premises accepted as-is; returned broom-clean at termination.

Insurance (Lessee obligation)

Lessee shall provide and maintain liability insurance for the mutual benefit of Lessee and Lessor: bodily injury / death $1,000,000 per occurrence and property damage $1,000,000 per occurrence. Lessor shall be named as an insured, and certificates of insurance shall be delivered to Lessor upon execution. Coverage may not be canceled or materially altered without 30 days' prior written notice to Lessor. Lessee insures its own contents. Parties mutually waive subrogation.

Build-out (Lessor, at no cost to Lessee)

  • Install sink in Office #2; cabinet & countertop in Office #2 (refrigerator by Lessee)
  • Repaint walls & trim (trim to remain existing color); re-carpet to existing quality
  • New tile in foyer & bathrooms; new solid-surface bathroom counters (selections by Lessor)
  • Wood door with glass insert at suite entrance

Other material terms

  • Signage: interior of Lessee's floor only; professional standard; listed on lobby directory as "SIA Investment Services."
  • Assignment / sublease: not without Lessor's prior written consent (not unreasonably withheld).
  • Holdover: month-to-month at Lessor's option; double rent if without consent.
  • Default: 10-day cure after notice; Lessor remedies include cancellation, acceleration of remaining rent, and re-letting.
  • Attorney's fees: 25% of amounts collected (min $100) plus court costs.
  • Casualty: automatic termination if rendered wholly unfit; rent abatement if repairable within 120 days.
  • Governing law: State of Louisiana.
  • Appliances supplied by Lessee. Floor plan, janitorial duty list, and park rules & regulations attached.
