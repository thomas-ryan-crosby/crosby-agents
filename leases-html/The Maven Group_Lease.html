Lease of Commercial Property

  1. Parties. Crosby Development Company, LLC, #1 Sanctuary Boulevard, Mandeville, LA 70471 (hereinafter "Lessor") hereby leases to The Maven Group, 128 Juniper Ct, Mandeville, LA 70471 (hereinafter "Lessee") the following described Leased Premises.
  2. Leased Premises.
    #1 Sanctuary Boulevard Floor 3, Suite 307 Mandeville, LA 70471 300 sqft Rentable Area
  3. In Solido Liability. If the above described Leased Premises is leased to more than one party, the obligations of all such parties hereunder, as lessees, shall be in solido.
  4. Term. The primary term of this lease shall be 36 months, commencing on November 24, 2025 and ending on November 24, 2028.
  5. Renewal. The lease agreement will automatically renew for 12 months at the expiration of the primary term and all renewal terms of Lessee. Lessee's right to terminate the term of this Lease is expressly conditioned upon Lessee delivering to Lessor written notice of exercise of such option not later than ninety (90) days prior to the expiration date of the primary term or the then renewal Term. All of the terms and conditions of this Lease shall remain in full force and effect during the renewal term except that there will be no further option or right to renew or extend the term of this Lease.
  6. Rental and Place of Payment. The rental under this lease shall be Five Hundred and fifty dollars ($550.00) monthly, payable in advance. Rent for the first full calendar month of the term, plus rent for any fractional preceding month, shall be payable on the signing of this lease by Lessee, and rent for subsequent months shall be payable on the first day of January 2026 and on the first day of each calendar month thereafter. All payments of rent shall be made to Lessor at #1 Sanctuary Boulevard, Mandeville, Louisiana 70471. Commencing with the first month of the second calendar year of the lease term, the rental shall increase three percent (3%) over the previous year's rent, applicable to all subsequent years including any renewal leases.
  7. Utility & Common Area Charges. All utility charges to be paid by Lessor. There are no common area charges or real estate taxes to be paid by the Lessee.
  8. Use of Premises. The leased premises shall be used only for the following purposes: General Office. The premises shall not be used for any unlawful purpose or in any manner that may damage or depreciate the same. Lessee shall comply with all requirements of State, Parish, Municipal, Federal, and other public authorities relating to use and occupancy.
  9. Build Out Specifications. Space to remain as is. Lessor to paint walls.
  10. Repairs and Maintenance. The leased premises and all appurtenances are accepted by Lessee in their present condition. Lessor shall maintain: (a) Roof; (b) Elevator; (c) Building Exterior and Parking Areas; (d) HVAC and Plumbing Systems; (e) Electrical Systems except fixtures; (f) Structural Components; (g) Janitorial Services per the Sanctuary Office Park Cleaning Schedule. Lessee shall, at Lessee's expense, make repairs to: (a) all interior finish surfaces including floors, walls and ceilings; (b) light bulb and light fixture replacement; (c) interior window cleaning. At termination, Lessee shall return the premises broom clean and deliver the keys to Lessor.
  11. Responsibility for Damages, Injuries, Losses. Lessor shall not be responsible for damage or injury caused by leaks unless Lessor fails to repair within a reasonable period after notice. Each party indemnifies the other for the negligence or willful misconduct of its agents/employees. The parties mutually release each other from subrogation claims, effective regardless of insurance coverage.
  12. Delayed Possession. Should Lessor be delayed in delivering possession, this lease shall not be affected; Lessee shall be allowed a remission of rent for the period prior to delivery of possession, and the termination date shall remain unchanged.
  13. Delay in Making Repairs. If Lessor deems it impracticable to complete improvements/repairs before commencement, Lessor may make them after possession is delivered, without reduction or remission of rent.
  14. Alterations or Additions by Lessee. Lessee shall not make alterations or additions without Lessor's prior written consent (not unreasonably withheld). Alterations become Lessor's property at termination unless otherwise agreed; Lessor may require removal and restoration.
  15. Insurance. Lessee shall maintain liability insurance of $1,000,000.00 for bodily injury and $1,000,000.00 for property damage per occurrence, naming Lessor as an insured, with certificates delivered upon execution. Lessee is responsible for insuring its own office contents.
  16. Acts of Lessee Affecting Insurance. Lessee shall pay any increased insurance cost due to its acts and shall not void coverage. Lessee shall notify Lessor when the premises will be unoccupied.
  17. Signs by Lessee. Lessee may erect signs within the interior of Lessee's floor, conforming to professional standards; signs to be removed at expiration. Lobby directory listing: The Maven Group.
  18. Right of Entry by Lessor. Lessor may enter at reasonable times to inspect and make repairs.
  19. For Sale and For Rent Signs; Inspection. Lessor may place "For Sale"/"By Auction" signs at any time and "For Rent" signs during the last six (6) months, and may show the premises to prospects.
  20. Surrender of Possession. Upon expiration or termination, Lessee shall surrender possession immediately. Holding over without written agreement may be treated as month-to-month at double the stipulated rent.
  21. Subleasing or Assignment. Lessee shall not sublease or assign without Lessor's prior written consent (not unreasonably withheld for a whole sublease or assignment).
  22. Damage by Fire or Other Casualty. If the premises are rendered wholly unfit by fire or casualty (not caused by Lessee), the lease terminates. If repairable within 120 days, Lessor may elect to repair with a proportionate rent remission.
  23. Default. On non-payment or breach (uncured after 10 days' notice), abandonment, insolvency, or bankruptcy, Lessor may, at its option: (1) cancel the lease immediately; (2) sue for past-due installments; (3) accelerate all unpaid rent for the unexpired term; or (4) pursue any other legal remedy. On cancellation, Lessor may re-enter and re-let, and Lessee remains liable for rent to the cancellation date plus damages for the remaining term.
  24. Attorney's Fees. If an attorney is engaged to enforce payment or protect Lessor's interests, Lessee agrees to pay a reasonable fee fixed at 25% of any money collected (not less than $100.00), plus court costs.
  25. Release of Lessor on Sale. Upon sale or transfer of the premises, the purchaser/transferee is bound for Lessor's obligations and the transferor is released from liability thereafter arising.
  26. Notices. Notices to Lessee are duly given if mailed registered/certified to the leased premises; notices to Lessor if mailed to the place where rent is paid. Either party may change its notice address by like written notice.
  27. Security Deposit. $500.00.
  28. Failure of Lessor to require strict performance on one or more occasions shall not constitute a waiver of the right to require strict compliance thereafter.
  29. This lease shall be deemed a contract under the laws of the State of Louisiana and construed accordingly, together with the ordinances of the municipality and parish where the premises are situated.
  30. All provisions are binding upon and inure to the benefit of the parties, their heirs, executors, administrators, successors, and assigns.
  31. The whole agreement is set forth in this instrument; the parties shall not be bound by any agreements, conditions, understandings, or representations other than those expressly set forth herein or in any amendments hereto.
