Third Amendment to Lease

THIS THIRD AMENDMENT TO LEASE (this “Amendment”) is made and entered into as of APRIL ___, 2025 (the “Effective Date”), by and between CROSBY DEVELOPMENT COMPANY, L.L.C., a Louisiana limited liability company (“Landlord”), and ASSUREDPARTNERS GULF COAST INSURANCE AGENCY, LLC, a Florida limited liability company (“Tenant”).

Recitals

  1. Landlord and Tenant are parties to that certain Lease of Commercial Property dated on or about July 1, 2014 (the “Original Lease”), as amended by that certain Amendment No. 1 to Lease dated January 23, 2019, and further amended by that Second Amendment to Lease dated April 8, 2022 (together, as amended, the “Lease”), whereby Landlord leases to Tenant office space located on Floor 2 commonly known as Suite 206 containing approximately 1,962 rentable square feet (the “Premises”), as more particularly described in Exhibit A attached hereto, within the building located at #2 Sanctuary Boulevard, Mandeville, LA 70471 (the “Building”), which is currently scheduled to expire on June 30, 2025 (the “Current Expiration Date”).
  2. Landlord and Tenant desire to extend the Lease Term and further amend the Lease on the following terms and conditions, as more particularly described in this Amendment.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements herein contained and other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, Landlord and Tenant agree as follows:

  1. Extension of Lease Term. As of the Effective Date hereof, the parties agree that the Lease Term for the Existing Premises is hereby modified and extended for an additional twelve (12) full calendar months commencing on July 1, 2025, so that the Expiration Date shall now be June 30, 2026. The period between July 1, 2025 (the “Extended Term Commencement Date”) and June 30, 2026, shall be referred to as the “Extended Term”, and all references in the Lease to the “Term” and the “Expiration Date” shall hereafter be amended as extended herein.
  2. Base Rent for the Premises. For the avoidance of doubt, Tenant shall continue to pay Base Rent for its use of the Premises under the currently existing Lease in monthly installments of $3,816.09 (“Current Base Rent”) through June 30, 2025. Commencing on July 1, 2025, and continuing through the Expiration Date, as extended herein, Base Rent for the Premises during the Extended Term shall be payable by Tenant in accordance with the terms of the Lease, as amended hereby, in the following amounts:
    Months Annual Base Rent Monthly Base Rent Annual Rent PSF
    Months 1 – 12
    (July 1, 2025 - June 30, 2026)    		 $46,107.00 $3,842.25 $23.50
  3. Additional Rent. Tenant shall continue only be responsible for paying Base Rent as provided above, except, except as expressly provided in the Lease. For the avoidance of doubt, Landlord shall continue to be responsible for all utility charges and confirms that there are no common area charges or real estate taxes to be paid by Tenant as provided in Section 6 of the Lease.
  4. Option to Renew. Tenant shall have one (1) additional option to renew the Lease for an additional period of two (2) years. Additional renewals shall incur a 3% annual Base Rent escalation. Lessee’s right to extend the term of this Lease is expressly conditioned upon (i) Lessee delivering to Lessor written notice of exercise of such option not later than six (6) months prior to the expiration date of this amendment term; and (ii) Lessee not being in default in the performance of any of its obligations under this Lease at the time of the exercise of the option and on the first day of the renewal term.
  5. Insurance. Section 14 of the Lease entitled “Insurance” shall hereby be amended and modified to clarify that, notwithstanding anything to the contrary set forth in Section 14 of the Lease, Landlord agrees that Tenant may carry all, or any portion of the required insurance coverage through blanket policies of insurance maintained by Tenant or any of its Affiliates (as defined in Section 6 below) covering the Premises and other locations of Tenant and its Affiliates in its portfolio of offices; provided, that Tenant or its Affiliate (i) Landlord and its parties shall be added as additional insureds to the applicable general liability and any umbrella or excess liability insurance policies; and (ii) the Premises shall be specifically identified or scheduled on any blanket policies of insurance.
  6. Subleasing or Assignment. Section 20 of the Lease shall hereby be amended and modified to provide, notwithstanding anything to the contrary set forth in the Lease, that Tenant may transfer all or part of its interest in the Lease or all or part of the Premises without Landlord’s prior written consent, but with written notice to Landlord (a “Permitted Transfer”) to: (a) an Affiliate (defined herein); (b) any corporation, limited partnership, limited liability partnership, limited liability company or other business entity in which or with which Tenant, or its corporate successors or assigns, is merged or consolidated, in accordance with applicable statutory provisions governing merger and consolidation of business entities, provided, that provided, that (i) Tenant’s obligations hereunder are assumed by the entity surviving such merger or created by such consolidation; or (c) any corporation, limited partnership, limited liability partnership, limited liability company or other business entity acquiring all or substantially all of Tenant’s assets. Tenant shall remain liable for the performance of all obligations of Tenant hereunder, or if the Tenant entity no longer exists because of a merger, consolidation or acquisition, the surviving or acquiring entity shall expressly assume in writing the obligations of Tenant hereunder. For purposes herein, the term “Affiliate” means any person or entity which, directly or indirectly, through one or more intermediaries, controls, is controlled by, or is under common control with Tenant.
  7. Notice Address. The Lease shall hereby be amended and modified to provide that any notices required to be delivered to Tenant pursuant to Section 25 of the Lease shall hereafter be addressed to Tenant as follows:

  8. Miscellaneous.
    1. This Amendment sets forth the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the matters set forth herein. There have been no additional oral or written representations or agreements. Under no circumstances shall Tenant be entitled to any rent abatement, improvement allowance, leasehold improvements, or other work to the Premises, or any similar economic incentives that may have been provided Tenant in connection with entering into the Lease, unless specifically set forth in this Amendment.
    2. Except as herein modified or amended, the provisions, conditions and terms of the Lease shall remain unchanged and in full force and effect and are hereby ratified and confirmed by Landlord and Tenant.
    3. In the case of any inconsistency between the provisions of the Lease and this Amendment, the provisions of this Amendment shall govern and control.
    4. Submission of this Amendment by Landlord is not an offer to enter into this Amendment but rather is a solicitation for such an offer by Tenant. Landlord shall not be bound by this Amendment until Landlord has executed and delivered the same to Tenant.
    5. The capitalized terms used in this Amendment shall have the same definitions as set forth in the Lease to the extent that such capitalized terms are defined therein and not redefined in this Amendment.
