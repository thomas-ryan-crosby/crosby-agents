Crosby Property Assistant — Tool & Capability Spec

The WhatsApp assistant answers by calling tools — small functions that fetch or compute exact figures straight from the live data — instead of guessing. Sections A and B are now built and live. Sections C and D are the remaining options — tick what you want and I'll build it.

A. Built & live today

These answer reliably right now — figures come from code, not the model's memory.

ToolWhat it answersExample question
find_tenantA tenant's lease facts: property/building/suite, SF, monthly rent, dates, escalation, auto-renew, notice, status"What does Wells Fargo pay? When does it end?"
get_lease_documentsLease & amendment documents for a tenant, with shareable PDF/HTML links"Send me the Pazos lease"
building_or_property_rentExact total monthly & annual base rent for a property or a specific building"Annual rent for Building 2?"
portfolio_totalsRent by property, portfolio total, highest-grossing property & building"What's our highest-grossing building?"
property_summaryOccupancy & rent summary for one property (SF/occupancy for commercial; units for apartments)"Occupancy at Mandeville Lake?"
expiring_leasesExpiring/vacating leases, soonest first, flagged vacating / auto-renew / fixed-term"What's vacating at Sanctuary this year?"
vacanciesVacant / available units, optionally per property"Any space available in Building 4?"
insurance_statusCertificate-of-insurance status (current / expiring / EXPIRED) per tenant"Which COIs are expired?"
monthly_rent_rollMonth-by-month (Jan–Dec) rent for a tenant, or monthly totals for a property/building"Give me the 2026 monthly rent roll for M Squared"
